# LinkedIn Growth Playbook — GTM Brain

Used by the recommendation engine and goal suggestion system to help users grow on LinkedIn.

## Algorithm in 2025-2026
- LinkedIn penalizes external links (30-40% reach reduction)
- Dwell time is the #1 signal — long posts that keep people reading win
- Comments weighted 12-15x a like (similar to X)
- Shares weighted 7x a like
- First 60-90 minutes determine reach (same as X)
- LinkedIn now favors "knowledge and advice" over personal stories (2024 algorithm update)
- Carousel/document posts get 2-3x reach vs text-only
- Video gets 5x reach but only if native (not YouTube links)

## The Hook — First 2 Lines
Everything depends on the first 2 lines (before "see more" cutoff). LinkedIn shows ~210 characters before the fold.

### Hook Formulas That Work
1. **Bold number**: "I said no to a $500K deal." / "87% of managers never received training."
2. **Vulnerable admission**: "I almost quit last month." / "I've been faking it for 3 years."
3. **Contrarian claim**: "Stop networking. It's a waste of time."
4. **Curiosity gap**: "The worst advice I ever got made me a millionaire."
5. **List tease**: "5 things I wish I knew before raising my Series A:"

## Post Formats Ranked by Reach
1. **Carousel/Document posts** — 2-3x reach, highest save rate
2. **Text-only with hook** — most consistent performer, 1-3 min read
3. **Native video** — 5x reach but needs to be good quality
4. **Polls** — high engagement but low quality followers
5. **Image + text** — moderate boost
6. **Posts with links** — 30-40% reach penalty (put links in comments)

## Comment Strategy (Inbound Growth)
Commenting on others' posts is the #1 growth hack on LinkedIn, same as X.

### Execution
1. Follow 20-30 high-profile creators in your niche
2. Comment within first 30 minutes of their posts
3. Write 2-4 sentence comments that add value (not "Great post!")
4. Target 10-15 meaningful comments per day
5. Your comment shows your name + headline to thousands of their followers
6. Expected: 200-500 new followers/month from commenting alone

### Comment Formulas
- "I've seen this firsthand. At [company], we tried X and found..."
- "This reminds me of [framework]. The missing piece is..."
- "Counterpoint: [contrarian angle]. Here's why..."
- Ask a specific follow-up question about their experience

## Posting Frequency
- Optimal: 3-5 posts per week (not per day like X)
- Monday and Tuesday have highest engagement
- Best times: 7-8 AM, 12-1 PM, 5-6 PM (audience timezone)
- Tuesday at 10 AM is the single highest-engagement slot
- Weekends: much lower reach (skip unless your audience is international)

## Daily Targets by Growth Stage

### 0-500 connections (Month 1-2)
- 3 posts/week
- 10 comments/day on bigger accounts
- Optimize headline: specific, outcome-focused ("I help X do Y")
- Connect with 10-20 people in your niche daily (with personalized notes)
- Weekly goal: 3 posts, 50 comments, 10 new connections

### 500-2,000 followers (Month 2-4)
- 4-5 posts/week
- 15 comments/day
- Start carousel posts (repurpose threads from X)
- Turn on creator mode
- Weekly goal: 5 posts, 75 comments, 1 carousel

### 2,000-10K followers (Month 4-12)
- 5 posts/week + 1 newsletter issue
- 15-20 comments/day
- Add native video (even 60-second clips)
- Launch LinkedIn newsletter (subscribers get push notifications)
- Weekly goal: 5 posts, 100 comments, 1 newsletter, 1 video

## Content That Performs
1. **"What I learned" posts** — Personal story + lesson. 800-1300 chars.
2. **Framework/system posts** — "My 3-step system for..." with numbered points
3. **Behind-the-scenes** — Share real numbers, real failures, real decisions
4. **Carousel tutorials** — Step-by-step guides as slide decks (use Canva)
5. **Contrarian takes** — Challenge industry conventional wisdom with evidence

## LinkedIn-Specific Growth Hacks
1. **Comment before posting**: Spend 15 min commenting, then post. Algorithm sees you as "active" and boosts your post.
2. **Edit within 10 minutes**: LinkedIn lets you edit. If you spot a typo, fix it. Edits don't hurt reach.
3. **Reply to every comment on your post** within 1 hour. Comments on your post boost its reach.
4. **Tag sparingly**: Max 3 tags. Excessive tagging looks spammy. Only tag people who will actually engage.
5. **Cross-post from X**: Your X threads become LinkedIn carousels. Different audience, same content.
6. **Profile banner = CTA**: Use your banner image to promote your newsletter, product, or free resource.
7. **Featured section**: Pin your best posts, newsletter, and any media appearances.
8. **Creator mode**: Turns "Connect" to "Follow" (lower friction for audience growth).

## Common Mistakes
1. **Posting links in main body** — 30-40% reach penalty. Put links in first comment.
2. **Posting too often** — Unlike X, 2+ posts/day on LinkedIn cannibalize each other's reach
3. **Generic comments** — "Great post!" / "Thanks for sharing!" / emoji-only = zero value
4. **Too promotional** — More than 1 in 5 posts being promotional kills engagement
5. **Wall of text** — No line breaks = no one reads past "see more"
6. **Ignoring comments** — Reply to every comment within 1 hour
7. **Hashtags overload** — Max 3-5, ideally 3. They matter less than on other platforms.
8. **Posting on weekends** — Unless your audience is global, weekends have 40-60% less reach
