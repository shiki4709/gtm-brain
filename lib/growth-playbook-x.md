# X/Twitter Growth Playbook — GTM Brain

Used by the recommendation engine and goal suggestion system to help users grow followers on X.

## The 70/30 Rule
Spend 70% of X time on strategic replies to bigger accounts, 30% creating original posts. This is the single highest-ROI activity for accounts under 10K.

## Algorithm Weights (from X's open-sourced code)
- Like = 0.5 weight
- Reply = 13.5 weight (27x a like)
- Reply where OP responds back = 75 weight (150x a like)
- Repost = 20x a like
- Bookmark = 10x a like
- Images = 2x engagement vs text-only
- Native video = up to 10x engagement
- X Premium = 4x in-network, 2x out-of-network visibility

## Algorithm Punishments
- External links in main tweet (deprioritized)
- Excessive hashtags (1-2 max)
- Negative/combative content (sentiment throttling)
- Spam-like behavior (20+ low-quality posts/day)
- Bot-like patterns (auto-likes, auto-replies)

## Reply Growth Strategy
1. Identify 20-30 large accounts in your niche (50K+ followers)
2. Turn on notifications for those accounts
3. Reply within first 5-15 minutes of their posts (early replies get surfaced to thousands)
4. Write replies that add value: personal anecdote, contrarian take, data point, or genuinely funny observation
5. Target 10-20 strategic replies per day
6. Expected: 500-1,000 new followers in 30 days with consistent execution

## Thread Strategy
- Threads get 3x more engagement than single tweets
- Threads generate up to 60% more profile visits
- Bookmarks (save-worthy content) = ~10x a like
- Post 2-3 threads per week
- Frameworks: "How I did X", curated lists, contrarian + proof, before/after, "steal my system"

## Quote Tweet Strategy
- Quote tweets carry ~20x a like in algorithmic weight
- 3-4 quote tweets per week
- Best: hot take on viral content, "here's what they're missing", signal boost + personal experience

## Daily Targets by Growth Stage

### 0-500 followers (Month 1-2)
- 3-5 original posts/day
- 10-20 strategic replies/day
- Join 2-3 X Communities
- Weekly goal: 5 replies/day, 1 thread/week

### 500-2,000 followers (Month 2-4)
- 5 posts/day + 15 replies/day
- 2-3 threads/week
- Start building in public
- Weekly goal: 10 replies/day, 2 threads/week

### 2,000-10K followers (Month 4-12)
- 5-7 posts/day + 15-20 replies/day
- Add video content
- Collaborate with other creators
- Weekly goal: 15 replies/day, 3 threads/week, 3 QTs/week

## Best Times to Post
- Weekdays: 8-10 AM and 7-9 PM (audience timezone)
- Weekends: 9-11 AM
- Avoid: 3-5 PM weekdays

## Growth Hacks
1. Self-reply chains: reply to your own tweet with a link or additional context
2. "Engage before you post" rule: 15-20 min engaging with others before publishing
3. First 30-60 min after posting determine reach. Engage with all replies within 15 min.
4. Put links in reply, never in main tweet
5. Post in X Communities (70K+ users join new communities daily)

## Common Mistakes
1. Generic replies ("Great post!") — zero growth value
2. No niche — pick ONE topic
3. AI-sounding language — audience can smell it
4. Inconsistent posting — disappearing kills algorithmic favor
5. Ignoring replies on own posts — conversations are 150x a like
6. Links in main tweet — algorithm buries them
